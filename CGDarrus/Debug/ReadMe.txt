Camera Controls:
W to rotate up
S to rotate down
A to rotate left
D to rotate right
E to zoom in
Q to zoom out

Up arrow key to move target camera up along the Y-Axis
Down arrow key to move target camera down along the Y-Axis
Left arrow key to move target camera left along the X-Axis
Right arrow key to move target camera right along the X-Axis

Light Controls:
I to move foward along the Z-Axis
K to move back along the Z-Axis
J to move left along the X-Axis
L to move right along the X-Axis
U to move down along the Y-Axis
O to move up along the Y-Axis

5 To use Point Lighting
6 To use Directional Lighting
7 To use Spotlight
